Solar System

~: Our Solar System :~

Our solar system consists of the Sun and a variety of celestial objects that orbit it. Let’s explore some key facts about our cosmic neighborhood.


The Sun: Our local star, the Sun, is at the center of the solar system. It provides light, heat, and energy to all the planets and other objects within its gravitational influence.


Planets: There are nine planets in our solar system. In order of their distance from the Sun:

  1. Mercury
  2. Venus
  3. Earth
  4. Mars
  5. Jupiter
  6. Saturn
  7. Uranus
  8. Neptune
  9. Pluto

Sun

  • Equator circumference: 4,379,000km.
  • Radius: 695,700km.
  • Temperature: 5,973°C to 15,000,000°C.
  • Average orbital speed around the Milky Way: 720,000km/h (200km/s)
  • Star type: Yellow dwarf.
  • Average time taken to rotate on axis: 27 Earth days.
  • Number of planets: 9
